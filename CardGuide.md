# DanModCards - Complete Card Guide

This guide documents all 24 cards in the DanModCards mod for ROUNDS, organized by category.

## Standard Cards

### 1. **Zoomies Burst**
Every few seconds you get a sudden speed and jump boost for a short duration. The timing is uncontrollable, and you don't decide when it happens.
- **Rarity:** Uncommon
- **Effects:** Speed Burst (+150%), Jump Burst (+100%), Random Timing

### 2. **Victor's Meow**
Emits a shockwave on block that knocks back nearby enemies. Grants an extra block charge.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Effects:** +1 Block charge, Shockwave on block activation

### 3. **Anti Gravity**
Nullifies gravity. You float freely through the air.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Effects:** Character Gravity -100%

### 4. **Infinity Mirror**
Every bullet you fire spawns 2 more bullets recursively with a short delay. Secondary bullets deal half damage. Maximum bullet cap: 30.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Effects:** 2 bullets spawned per shot (with delay), Secondary damage -50%, Max 30 bullets

### 5. **Spray++**
Spray and pray – fire at blinding speed with tons of ammo, but each bullet hits softer. Fires continuously while holding the fire button.
- **Rarity:** Uncommon
- **Stats:** Attack Speed +1000%, Ammo +36, Damage -75%

### 6. **Rail Cannon**
Fires a devastatingly fast, zero-drop projectile that sends enemies flying. Clip is small but the impact is enormous.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Projectile Speed +600%, Knockback +100%, Recoil +100%, Ammo -2, Gravity -100%, Drag -25%

### 7. **Mega Tank**
Become an unstoppable wall of health. You're nearly impossible to kill – just don't expect to move quickly.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Health +300%, Character Gravity +30%, Movement Speed -50%

### 8. **Landmines**
Bullets drop straight to the ground and detonate for massive damage. Fire and jump away!
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Bullet Gravity +1500%, Bullet Speed -75%, Damage +400%

### 9. **Glass Dildo**
You shatter at the slightest touch, but your weapon hits like a freight train.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Health -99%, Damage +1000%

### 10. **Can't Catch Me**
You're a blur. Nobody can catch you now.
- **Rarity:** Uncommon
- **Stats:** Movement Speed +200%

## Adult-Themed Cards

### 11. **Cock and Load**
Load up your gun… and your partner. Massive magazine, but you gotta take your sweet time reloading.
- **Rarity:** Common
- **Stats:** Max Ammo +200%, Reload Time +120%

### 12. **Big Dick Energy**
Your gun gets THICC. Massive damage and bullet size, but you can barely aim with all that confidence.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Damage +200%, Bullet Size +150%, Spread +120%

### 13. **Deep Throat**
Swallow those bullets whole. Insane speed and pierce, but your aim gets a little sloppy at the end.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Bullet Speed +180%, Pierce +4, Spread +100%

### 14. **Ball Buster**
Bust balls on sight. Enemies take huge damage… but every shot kicks you right in the nuts too.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Damage +250%, Self Damage on Fire 30%, Recoil +200%

### 15. **Reverse Cowgirl**
Ride it backwards for maximum confusion. Your bullets bounce like crazy — nobody knows who's getting fucked anymore.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Bounce +6, Bullet Speed +120%, Spread -60%

### 16. **The Unlubed Dildo**
No lube. No mercy. Raw, brutal penetration. It hurts everyone except you.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Damage +400%, Bullet Speed +250%, Reload Time +180%, Player Size +80%

### 17. **Cumshot Cannon**
One massive, sticky explosion that paints the whole room. You only get one shot… then you're spent for the rest of the round.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Damage +500%, Max Ammo -95%, Fire Rate -70%, Bullet Size +300%

### 18. **Balls Deep**
Go all the way in. Bullets travel forever and pierce through everything… including your own dignity when you miss.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Pierce +8, Bullet Speed +300%, Spread +150%, Bullet Gravity -90%

### 19. **Edging Master**
Hold it… hold it… HOLD IT… NOW BUST. Charge up for god-tier damage or ruin everything by firing too early.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Damage +350%, Fire Rate -85%, Max Ammo -60%

### 20. **Creampie Deluxe**
Fill every hole and leave a sticky mess behind. Enemies move slower after getting filled… just like in real life.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Damage +220%, Bullet Speed +140%, Enemy Slow on Hit 70%, Max Ammo -40%

### 21. **Throat Goat Supreme**
The GOAT at taking it all the way down. Insane range and pierce, but your aim gets throat-fucked at the end.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Pierce +6, Bullet Speed +400%, Spread +180%, Player Gravity +200%

### 22. **Nut Buster 9000**
Busts nuts so hard you'll hear the echo. Massive damage + self-recoil that launches you into orbit.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Damage +300%, Recoil +500%, Bullet Size +200%, Self Damage on Fire 25%

### 23. **Double Penetration**
Why use one when you can use two? Double the bullets in sequence, double the fun… double the absolute chaos.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Extra Projectiles +2 (burst), Spread +200%, Damage -40%, Bounce +4

### 24. **Backdoor Bandit**
Sneaky backshots that curve around corners and hit where it hurts most. You can't see shit, but neither can they.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Stats:** Bounce +5, Bullet Speed +180%, Spread +100%, Movement Speed +100%
